WELCOME

I am a final year student at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Bhopal pursuing a BS-MS Degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science with a minor in Biology. Currently I am doing my master’s thesis in i-Lab under the supervision of Dr. Mitradip Bhattacharjee (IISER Bhopal - Dept. of EECS).. My research interests include electronic sensors, biomedical engineering, wearable electronics, flexible/printed bioelectronics, and soft robotics.

I have worked with Prof. Geroge K. Knopf (Univeristy of Western Ontario - Dept. of Mechanical and Materials Engineering) and Dr.Areejit Samal (IMSc Chennai - Dept. of Computational Biology) during the MITACS GRI Summer research fellowship and IAS Summer Research Fellowship, respectively.

I love reading (fiction, non-fiction, and mystery) and exercising during my leisure time.
I also enjoy organizing events and have done quite a few during my tenure as Secretary, Cultural Council of IISER Bhopal (2020-2021).
